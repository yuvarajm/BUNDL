What Data Can Families Share With You?

Bundl's data sharing is parent-controlled and privacy-first. Families choose what to share, when, and with whom. You receive clean, readable reports.

Comprehensive Wellness Reports

The Bundl app generates shareable health summaries that can be exported as PDF or sent directly to a care provider via secure link. Reports include configurable date ranges and all monitored vital categories.

  • Heart rate averages, ranges, and trend lines by week
  • SpO₂ daily averages with below-threshold event log
  • Skin temperature trends with overheating event flags
  • Movement and activity patterns by time of day
  • Sleep architecture breakdown: deep, light, and REM phases
  • Total sleep duration and night waking frequency
  • Alert history with timestamps and parental responses logged
  • Band wear compliance (hours/day monitored)

All data exports include a prominent disclaimer that Bundl is a wellness monitor, not a medical device, and that readings should be interpreted in clinical context.
