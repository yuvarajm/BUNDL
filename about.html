About Us

Built by Students,
Driven by Purpose.

A four-member student-led startup from Liberty Hill, Texas, building technology that helps families feel a little more supported.

Why We Started Bundl

As students, we noticed how stressful and emotionally exhausting it can be for parents, especially parents of infants, to constantly worry about their baby's comfort, safety, and overall well-being throughout the day and night.

Many existing baby-monitoring and wellness products are expensive and out of reach for a lot of families. We want to help create a future where parents don't have to spend overwhelming amounts of money just to feel informed and reassured about their child's well-being.

💛

"We started Bundl because peace of mind for parents should not feel like a luxury."

— The Bundl Team

Our Mission & Values

Everything we build, every decision we make, begins with a single question: does this help parents feel more informed and less afraid?

🔬

Science First

Every feature, every threshold, every alert is grounded in pediatric research and validated clinical data. We never make health claims we can't support.

❤️

Parent-Centered Design

We design for exhausted parents at 3 AM, not for tech reviewers. Clarity over complexity. Calm over alarm. Information that empowers, not overwhelms.

🔒

Privacy by Default

Your baby's health data belongs to you. We use strong encryption, collect only what's necessary, and never sell data to third parties. Ever.

🌿

Sustainably Made

GOTS-certified organic cotton, TENCEL Lyocell from sustainably managed forests, and minimal packaging. Good for your baby, good for the planet.

Built with Care for the Planet

Sustainable isn't a marketing word for us; it's a responsibility. Your baby will inherit this world. We take that seriously.

🌱

GOTS Certified

Our organic cotton is certified by the Global Organic Textile Standard, the world's leading standard for organic fibers.

🌲

TENCEL Lyocell

Sourced from sustainably managed forests using a closed-loop production process that recovers and reuses 99% of solvents.

🐾

Animal-Free

No animal-derived materials in any Bundl product. All materials are certified cruelty-free and free from harmful chemical treatments.

📦

Minimal Packaging

Our packaging uses recycled cardboard and soy-based inks. No unnecessary plastic. Designed to be reused or composted.

♻️

Band Recycling

When your baby outgrows a band size, send it back. We'll recycle the materials through our certified textile recovery partners.

🌍

Carbon Offsetting
