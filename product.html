Our Technology

2-Step Verification System

Peace of Mind, Verified.

Because one unusual reading shouldn't send parents into panic.

Traditional monitoring systems often trigger alerts from a single irregular reading, even if it's caused by movement, positioning, or temporary sensor interference. Bundl introduces an advanced Two-Step Verification System designed to reduce unnecessary false alarms and give parents more trustworthy notifications.

Bundl's Verification Flow

⚡

Irregular Vital Detected

A reading falls outside expected healthy range

↓
🔬

Cross-Sensor Analysis

Irregular reading verified across multiple sensor inputs simultaneously

↓
🔄

Consistent Confirmation

System confirms the irregularity persists, not a sensor glitch or movement artifact

↓
✅

Verified Alert Sent

Parent receives a meaningful, actionable notification

Materials

Triple-Layer Comfort System

Every layer is chosen for your baby's delicate skin. Safe. Breathable. Certified.

1

TENCEL™ Lyocell: Outer Layer

Botanical fibre from eucalyptus. 50% more breathable than cotton, naturally temperature-regulating, zero skin irritants.

2

Bamboo Cotton: Middle Layer

Moisture-wicking and naturally antibacterial. Pulls sweat away from skin and stays odour-free through full-day wear.

3

Organic Cotton: Inner Layer

GOTS certified, grown without pesticides. The softest layer sits directly against your baby's ankle skin.

Personalized Insights

Your Baby's Data, Turned Into Guidance.

Babies come with a future full of possibilities, adorable noises, and unforgettable moments.   But the one thing they don't come with… is an instruction manual.

Bundl transforms everyday health data into personalized baby care insights designed specifically for your child. Instead of overwhelming parents with numbers and charts alone, Bundl helps translate patterns into meaningful, actionable guidance.

😴

Sleep Pattern Insights

Understand your baby's sleep cycles (light sleep, deep sleep, and wake windows) so you can build healthier routines.

💛

Comfort & Stress Trends

Track how your baby's vitals shift throughout the day to better understand what keeps them calm and comfortable.

🌿

Personalized Baby Care Tips

Receive gentle, data-informed suggestions tailored to your baby's unique patterns, not one-size-fits-all advice.

📋

Daily Wellness Summaries

A simple daily overview of your baby's health trends so you always know how their day looked at a glance.

📈

Smart Health Trend Tracking

Spot patterns over days and weeks. See how your baby grows and evolves so nothing catches you off guard.

🏥

Pediatrician-Ready Reports

Share comprehensive vital trend reports directly with your doctor for more informed, productive check-up conversations.

Every baby is different. That's why Bundl's insights adapt to your baby's unique patterns, helping parents better understand routines, comfort levels, sleep quality, and overall wellness over time.
